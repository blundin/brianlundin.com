Quick Grades

The simple, fast, and elegant easy grader.

Quick Grades is the free easy grading app designed for busy teachers like you. The clean design is easy to read at a glance. You can choose which columns you want to see, and which ones you don't. The beautiful and uncluttered design is easy on your eyes during those long grading sessions.

Quick Grades is designed from the ground up for iOS 12 to bring you a simple, elegant, reliable experience on all supported iPhones.
